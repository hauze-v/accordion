Fun facts about One Punch Man

How did Saitama get his unparalleled powers?

+

An intense exercise regimen.

What's the secret to his intense exercise regimen?

+

100 push-ups, 100 sit-ups, 100 squats, and a 10km run. EVERY SINGLE DAY!!!

Why is Saitama bald?

+

He literally trained so hard he lost all his hair.

What does Saitama hate more than missing bargains at the local market?

+

Bugs.

What are some of his hobbies aside from being an average hero?

+

Reading manga! Young Saitama has an entire bookshelf full of comics, and his younger self was a big fan of Mob Pyscho.

Why does Saitama typically interrupt people speaking to him?

+

He has limited paitence for long explanations. His ideal word count is 20 or less.
